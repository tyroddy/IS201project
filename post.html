My Journey Through Diverse Roles and Industries

Professional Experience

I have had the privilege of working at Walt Disney World, where I honed my exceptional people skills and provided outstanding customer service in a dynamic, fast-paced environment. Currently, I am employed at Olive Garden, where I continue to excel in delivering top-notch service and creating positive dining experiences for guests.

My background in both of these roles has equipped me with a strong ability to connect with customers, resolve issues effectively, and ensure a high level of satisfaction, all of which are crucial for success in customer-facing positions.

Extracurricular Experience

During high school, I demonstrated leadership and teamwork as the captain of both the swim and water polo teams. I was also one of the founders of the water polo team, playing a pivotal role in establishing and growing the program. These positions allowed me to develop strong organizational skills, foster team spirit, and mentor fellow athletes. My involvement in these extracurricular activities reflects my dedication, initiative, and ability to lead and inspire others in achieving common goals.

I also had the honor of serving as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, where I dedicated two years to community service, teaching, and leadership. During my mission, I developed strong communication and interpersonal skills, managed diverse responsibilities, and worked effectively with individuals from various cultural backgrounds. This experience profoundly enhanced my ability to connect with others, work under pressure, and demonstrate unwavering commitment and integrity in all my endeavors.
